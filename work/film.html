CONVERGENCIA (2020)

CONVERGENCIA (CONVERGENCE) is a collective, participatory film project developed during quarantine, gathering audiovisual material from 12 people all across Bolivia, to manifest our lifestyles from a non-anthropocentric lense.
Focusing on the body as an object of investigation and working tool, this dynamic was facilitated through a cross-pollinated insight between body, word, camera and image. The project was worked wholly digital manner, facilitating dialogues and the production of material through online conferences.

CONVERGENCE was possible with the funding support of the Municipal Funds for Arts and Culture FOCUART 2020, La Paz, Bolivia.

hola casita (2020)

Experimental short-film commissioned by Trinity Laban Conservatoire of Music and Dance for their Isolation Series during the quarantine.
Responding to the experience of lockdown in my hometown of La Paz, Bolivia, the film welcomes the viewer into moments of intimacy between a home and its inhabitants. Translated as ‘hello little house’, the project portrays part of the process of my home-coming after studying abroad for five years. It is a collage of rediscovery and experience.

The film is a poetic registry of the everyday interactions that happen in a territory defined by the walls of a house. Amidst a complex global crisis, that is felt locally and personally, the project aims to bridge multiple layers of socio-political action that are urgent and possible, from the soil to the computer.
While decentralizing the human from the concept of 'home', the project aims to inspire dancers and non-dancers to think of the framing of their everyday lives as a tool for self-investigation and introspection. The film encourages people to understand that living is the fundamental job, in addition to how our ability to perceive is our most important communicative tool.
